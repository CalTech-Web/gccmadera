## History

- **Founded:** 1977
- **Originally named:** Madera Community Church
- **Incorporated:** May 1978 (as Madera Community Church)
- **Name changed to:** Grace Community Church of Madera (shortly after 1978 incorporation)
- **Origins:** Began as a home study → privately run business → community hall
- **Early pastoral support:** Northwest Church of Fresno provided assistance for morning services
- **1980:** Called first pastor
- **1983:** Randy Brannon moved from Portland, Oregon to assume pastoral responsibilities; John Adams School became church service site
- **October 1985:** Moved to current campus (congregation built worship center over 5 months, financed through internal church notes)
- **1989/90:** Congregation constructed the educational building
- **1987:** Missions ministry began with first Faith Promise Missions Conference
- **April 2019:** James Kendall moved from North Carolina to become Senior Pastor
- **December 2021:** James Kendall ended tenure as Senior Pastor
- **January 2022:** Brandon Hopkins assumed role of Interim Senior Pastor
- **June 6, 2022:** Members voted in Brandon Hopkins as Senior Pastor

## Beliefs Summary

### Statement of Faith (Key Points)
1. **Holy Scriptures** — Verbally inspired Word of God; inerrant in original writings; final authority (2 Tim 3:16–17; 2 Pet 1:20–21)
2. **The Godhead** — One Triune God; Father, Son, Holy Spirit; co-eternal, co-identical in nature, co-equal in power (Deut 6:4; 2 Cor 13:14)
3. **Person & Work of Christ** — Eternal Son of God; born of Virgin Mary; redemption through death on cross; bodily resurrection; ascended, interceding at right hand of God
4. **Person & Work of the Holy Spirit** — Convicts of sin; regenerates; baptizes believers into body of Christ; indwells and seals believers
5. **Total Depravity of Man** — Man fell in Adam; sinful nature; unable to remedy his lost condition
6. **Salvation** — Gift of God by grace through personal faith in Jesus Christ; His blood shed for forgiveness

### Baptism
- Believers' baptism only (not infant baptism)
- Mode: Immersion only (based on Greek *baptizo* = "to dip/submerge")
- Infant dedication ceremony offered for Christian parents
- To arrange baptism: call (559) 674-1172 or sign up online

### Marriage
- Permanent, lifelong covenant between one man and one woman
- Homosexuality, lesbianism, bisexuality, and other sexual immorality are considered sinful perversions
- Opposes gender alteration by surgery or appearance
- Two biblical grounds for divorce: sexual sin or desertion by an unbeliever
- Weddings at GCC are considered Christian worship services

### Communion
- Memorial meal; not transubstantiation
- Elements: Bread (wafer cracker) and grape juice
- Participants: Believers in Christ who have examined themselves
- Practiced as gathered church

### Church Membership
- Considered an important step of commitment for believers
- Member rights: Vote to confirm new elders, constitutional changes, property purchases
- To pursue membership: come forward after a service, fill out welcome card, call office, or fill out online form
